Datasets

ages.csv
--------
-presidential ages at inauguration 
-source: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Presidents_of_the_United_States_by_age

brush-floss.xls
---------------
-brush vs. floss frequencies of 3,610 individuals from Baltimore, Maryland, San Antonio, Texax, and Sioux and Lakota communities
-source: http://www.stat.ucla.edu/cases/

doctor-*.xls
------------
-frequency distributions of number of office visits to a doctor or other health care
professional in the past 12 months among persons 18 years of age and over 
(United States, 2008)
-source: http://www.cdc.gov/nchs/data/series/sr_10/sr10_242.pdf

enrollment.xls
---------------
-school enrollment status of youths during the October when ages 19 in 
1997-2004, by sex (percent distribution)
-Source: http://www.bls.gov/spotlight/2007/back_to_school/data.htm#table3

fish-prices.xls
---------------
-prices in cents per pound received by fisherman and vessel owners for various species of fish and shellfish in 1970 and 1980
-source: http://lib.stat.cmu.edu/DASL/Datafiles/FishPrices.html

labor.xls
---------
-labor force participation rate of women in 19 cities in the United States in 1968
and 1972
-source: http://lib.stat.cmu.edu/DASL/Stories/WomenintheLaborForce.html

magazines.xls
-------------
-readability levels of magazine ads based on reader's educational levels
-magazines-wds: WDS = number of wrds
-magazines-sen: SEN = number of sentences
-magazines-3syl: 3SYL = number of 3+ syllable words 
-source: http://lib.stat.cmu.edu/DASL/Datafiles/magadsdat.html

reading.xls
-----------
-Degree of Reading Power (DRP) test scores measuring the aspects of reading ability
that directed reading activities in the classroom are designed to improve
-source: http://lib.stat.cmu.edu/DASL/Datafiles/DRPScores.html

sadness.xls
-----------
-degree of sadness vs. gender
-source: http://www.cdc.gov/nchs/data/series/sr_10/sr10_242.pdf

salary.xls
----------
-salary rates of university professors
-source: http://lib.stat.cmu.edu/DASL/Datafiles/FacultySalaries.html

suicide-gender.xls
------------------
-U.S. suicides in 1983 by gender and method
-source: http://www.stat.ucla.edu/cases/

thatch-ant.csv
--------------
-thatch-ant data for foraging habits study: colony, foraging distance, mass, headwidth,
worker class
-source: http://www.stat.ucla.edu/data/

titanic.xls
-----------
-Titanic disaster casualty figures
-source: http://www.anesi.com/titanic.htm
